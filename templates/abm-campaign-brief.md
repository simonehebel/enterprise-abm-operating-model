# ABM Campaign Brief

## Purpose

This template supports the planning and execution of an account-based marketing campaign.

It helps Sales and Marketing align on:

- the business objective,
- target accounts,
- customer needs,
- buying committee priorities,
- messaging,
- engagement tactics,
- ownership,
- follow-up,
- measurement.

The brief should be completed before campaign activation and reviewed jointly by Sales and Marketing.

## Key Principle

An ABM campaign should not be a collection of disconnected tactics.

It should connect:

- business priorities,
- account decisions,
- customer needs,
- buying committee relevance,
- Sales and Marketing ownership,
- engagement,
- pipeline progression,
- measurable commercial outcomes.
