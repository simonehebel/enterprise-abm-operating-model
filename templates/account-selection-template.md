# Account Selection Template

## Purpose

This template supports a structured and transparent selection of target accounts for an account-based marketing program.

It helps Sales and Marketing evaluate accounts based on:

- strategic fit,
- commercial potential,
- customer need,
- relationship strength,
- buying readiness,
- execution feasibility.

The goal is to prioritize accounts where focused investment is justified and operationally realistic.

## How to Use This Template

Complete one assessment for each account under consideration.

Use available evidence and clearly distinguish between:

- confirmed facts,
- informed assumptions,
- open questions,
- missing information.

The assessment should be reviewed jointly by Sales and Marketing before the account is assigned to an ABM tier.

## Key Principle

A target account should not be prioritized because it is large or well known.

Focused ABM investment should be based on:

- strategic relevance,
- customer need,
- commercial value,
- buying readiness,
- Sales commitment,
- execution feasibility.
